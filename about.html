About Us

Discover the luxury behind Fred'Rache Staffing

Our Luxury Heritage

Fred'Rache provides hosts and waitstaff for events like weddings, birthdays, church gatherings, concerts, and both public and private functions. Based in Leeds, UK, we offer our services worldwide.

Our journey began with a passion for exceptional service and a commitment to elevating the standard of event staffing. Our motto, "Warm Welcome avec MA'JOIE," reflects our approach – bringing joy and excellence to every event.
